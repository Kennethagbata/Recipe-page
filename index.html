Simple Omellet Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omellet combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 mins
  • Preparation: 5 mins
  • Cooking: 5 mins

Ingridents

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 table spoon of butter or Oil
  • Optiona fillings: Cheese, dicied vegetable, cooking meat, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture.
  2. Heat the pan: place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the Omellet: Once the butter is melted and bubbling, pour in the egg. Tilt the pan to ensure the egg evenly coats the surface.
  4. Add fillings (optional): When the egges begin to set at the edges but are still slightly runny at the middle, sprinkle your choosen fillings over one half of the omellet.
  5. Fold and serve: As the omellet continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it over the slings. Let it cook for another minute then slide it unto a plate.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot with additional salt and pepper if needed.
